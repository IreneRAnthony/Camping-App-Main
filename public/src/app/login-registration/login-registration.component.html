The Traveling Bard

For all those with a wandering eye to the distant horizon, a insatiable wanderlust, or even just the curiosity to try something different, you have found yourself in the right direction. As the purpose of this site is to help make all your camping experiences just a little easier. Based on the story of a traveling bard and her journey with her companions, this website is designed to help you create task lists, plan trips, and make headway into starting your own adventure.
